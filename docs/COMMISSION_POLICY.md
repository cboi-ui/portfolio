# Commission Policy

## Process

1. Client reaches out on Discord with project details.
2. Scope and requirements are discussed.
3. Price and timeline are agreed upon.
4. Work begins after confirmation/payment terms are agreed.
5. Preview is shared with the client.
6. Final delivery.

## What clients should provide up front

- Game/project description
- UI requirements
- Style references
- Deadline
- Preferred payment method

## Revisions

Each project includes a limited number of revisions (defined at quote time).
Additional revisions beyond that may require extra payment.

## Payment

- Robux or USD, depending on availability of payment method.
- Terms (upfront/split/on-delivery) are agreed before work starts.
